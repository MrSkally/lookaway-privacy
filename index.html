LookAway – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

No data collected

LookAway does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

Local storage only

Your preferences (notification type and reminder interval) are saved locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your browser and is never sent to any server.

No analytics or tracking

LookAway contains no analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party scripts of any kind.

No servers

LookAway has no backend. It operates entirely within your browser.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on the GitHub repository.
